Our Team

Our dynamic group of talented and passionate people are eagerly creating the most immersive virtual reality experience ever. They strive toward innovative and amazing experiences that change the way people view the world. Their goal is to push the boundary of creative production in the emerging field of virtual/augmented reality in an independent, experimental and design driven environment. They are critical thinkers, creatives, artists, marketers, problem solvers, and doers. Our vision first approach is taking the virtual-reality experience Beyond.

Our Mission

Our mission is to capture your imagination and take your dreams to a place beyond the scope of the human capacity of vision. Our motto is "Aut viam inveniam aut faciam." That is if we can not find a way, we will make one. Beyond Vision is leading the way toward a new way to move through life. Adventurers can now step beyond their reality into one of infite possibilities: places where dreams and nightmares have substance, hopes and fears can be touched and worlds without number await to be explored.
