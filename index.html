Beyond Vision

Beyond Vision is a platform for building virtual-reality and augmented-reality experiences. They are focused on acquiring and onboarding enterprise-level clients. Their image is sleek, high-end, and reliable.

Ocular, which is not only related to vision in general but also another word meaning eyepiece, and prime as another word for first. So Ocular Prime is the sophisticated eyepiece which you must first look through to create a synergized blend of technological engineering that is Beyond Vision. Vision first is the approach which speaks to what I believe this company would like to convey.
